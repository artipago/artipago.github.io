Welcome to my website!
I’m currently a CNRS researcher at the Brain and Cognition Research Center (CerCo) in Toulouse (France).
Before that, I was a postdoc under the supervision of Rufin VanRullen (CerCo) and Thomas Serre (Brown University).
Before that, I received my PhD at the Université Catholique de Louvain (Belgium), under the supervision of Alexandre Zénon and Etienne Olivier.
Before that, I completed a bachelor and master degree in Biomedical Engineering in Italy (Politecnico di Milano).
Currently, my research interests revolve around Cognitive Neuroscience, using both Experimental and Computational approaches.
